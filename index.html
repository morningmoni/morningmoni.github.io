About Me

Hi, human / crawler!

I am Yuning Mao , a fourth-year Ph.D student in Prof. Jiawei Han's Data Mining Group, Dept. of Computer Science, University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign (UIUC). I graduated from the IEEE Honored Class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).

My research centers on helping humans to acquire information and knowledge from unstructured texts more effectively and efficiently via natural language processing, machine learning, and data mining. I have experience in topics such as text summarization, question answering, semantic matching, taxonomy construction, hierarchical classification, and knowledge graph reasoning.

You can find some fun facts about me here, most of which are not in my CV (cuz I update it mainly for fun:)
